Autotrol Valve Series & Technologies

Autotrol 360 Valve: A manual filter valve designed for straightforward, hands-on control in filtration systems.

Autotrol 361 Valve: A semi-automatic filter valve that combines manual operation with automated features for enhanced efficiency.

Autotrol 367 Valve: A softener control valve optimised for precise management of water softening processes, delivering exceptional performance.

Duraflow Flapper Technology: Engineered for reliability, the Duraflow flapper delivers a frictionless seal, reducing wear and extending service life while enhancing overall system performance.

Superior Valve Construction: Built with a fibre-reinforced polymer body, these valves offer exceptional strength, durability, and resistance to corrosion and UV exposure, making them perfect for challenging environments.

Features & Benefits

  • Manual & Semi-Automatic Operation: No programming required, offering straightforward control for efficient filtration.
  • High Flow Rates: Continuous service flow rate of 21 GPM, with a backwash flow rate of 19 GPM (full flow).
  • User-Initiated Regeneration: Easily activate regeneration with a simple knob.
  • Single Tank System: Designed for single-tank use, delivering consistent performance.
  • Flow Options: Available in full flow or low flow versions to suit various requirements.
